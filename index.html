The Sqorl Collection
Bold. Brash. Uncompromising. All of these adjectives describe Sqorl's style, yet no words could ever fully capture the heartstring-tugging expressiveness of his work.
"Keqing" - 8 September 2021
Sqorl's art world debut features a Genshin favorite sporting a fashionable winter sweater, a purple scissor blade complete with floating noodles, and ears that totally do not belong to a cat.
"Gold Star" - 20 October 2021
Created as a token of appreciation for Mephis's willingness to lend us a Priconne account for CB#9, Sqorl's second work is a testament to the dedication and care he shows for his craft. Here, Sqorl has opted to manually paint a uniquely shaped star, as opposed to drawing a perfectly symmetrical one using a pre-built shape function that any basic editing program would have.
"Christmas Present" - 25 December 2021
Sqorl expresses the love he has for his clan members.
"Happy New Year" - 31 December 2021
Sqorl's last work of 2021 is a celebratory piece commemorating the arrival of a new year. This one-of-a-kind image depicts two maracas with white decorative strings on the handles, a pair of green spider plants, and one of those weird worm monsters from the first Men in Black movie.
"Twitch Prime" - 7 February 2022
Sqorl displays a strangely intense affinity for Twitch Prime.
"Mage CB" - 21 March 2022
Here, Bshandon is shown wearing a green traffic cone for a hat while sitting on a chair crying, presumably because someone surgically removed his kidney and placed it next to him. Meanwhile, his fellow clan members display an incredible amount of limb flexibility and dance in a shower of asbestos.
"Dinosaur" - 15 May 2022
Sqorl presents his own take on the highly questionable costume prop that someone had posted in the Discord just moments earlier.
